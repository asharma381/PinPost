Current Issue

Unconstained Vegetation Growth

Northern California has been plagued by hundreds of wildfires, many of which are sparked by malfunctions in the power grid. Utility companies like PG&E have utility lines spanning thousands of miles and routine checks on each of these lines can only happen once a year. As such, vegetation around utility poles can come into contact with power lines.
